Frequently Asked Questions

Is CaryRx a pharmacy?

Yes, we distribute directly from our pharmacy located in Washington, DC.
‍
CaryRx’s founders have backgrounds in both pharmacy and technology. We built CaryRx because you deserve a pharmacy that can provide an amazing mobile experience.

You will love your new pharmacy.

Where do you deliver? Is delivery free?

Free scheduled same-day delivery is available to all of Washington, DC, and parts of Maryland and Northern Virginia. Scheduled deliveries must be confirmed by no later than 1pm. The following map shows our same-day delivery coverage.

The darker blue shared area on the map shows locations eligible for ‘express’ delivery, our fastest delivery option available where orders can be delivered on-demand, typically within two hours!

CaryRx is also able to ship nationwide. Please note: We are unable to deliver / ship controlled substance medications outside of Washington DC, Maryland, and Virginia. You’ll see all delivery options available to your location when placing an order in the CaryRx app.

Do you deliver controlled substances?

We have currently reached our regulatory limit for accepting new patients requiring schedule II controlled substances, such as Adderall and Vyvanse.

We understand the importance of these medications and recognize the impact this may have on our patients. Our team is actively working within regulatory guidelines to explore options and potentially expand our capacity in the future.

Do you ship refrigerated medications such as WeGovy and Mounjaro?

At this time, we are unable to ship refrigerated medications beyond the areas of DC, MD, and VA. Unfortunately, we cannot guarantee the required cold chain storage temperatures during transit.

We understand the importance of these medications, and our team is working diligently to find solutions. Currently, we can deliver/mail non-refrigerated and non-controlled medications to our patients.

Do you take my insurance?

CaryRx accepts the majority of plans, including medicare.

Will my copay be the same?

When filling prescriptions with CaryRx your copay will be the same as any other pharmacy.

Can I order for my family or pet?

You can order for your family members or pets by adding them in the “Family” tab in “My Account.”

How do I fill prescriptions with CaryRx?

Select “+” to view options for adding a prescription to your CaryRx account. They include:

1. Transfer prescriptions from another pharmacy by clicking “I would like to transfer from a pharmacy”.

2. Have your doctor send your prescriptions directly to CaryRx and you will receive a notification when it is ready to order. Click “My doctor will send it.” to see our pharmacy information.

Once your doctor sends a prescription to CaryRx, you will get a push notification indicating your prescription is ready to be ordered.

Are packages sealed?

All our packages are securely sealed by our pharmacists after they perform the final check.

Who are CaryRx’s pharmacists?

All of CaryRx’s pharmacists are licensed pharmacists with doctorates in pharmacy. They are all extremely knowledgeable, so send them a chat and ask them questions about your medications or health!

Can I talk to a pharmacist?

Members of the CaryRx pharmacy team are available via in-app chat. To get started, download the CaryRx mobile app and tap on the chat button, located on the bottom right hand corner of the menu bar. We'll be happy to help you!

What if my medication isn’t in stock?

While we have an expansive inventory, there will be times when a medication will have to be restocked. If we don’t have your medication, it can normally be in-stock on the next business day. We'll send you a push notification as soon we have it, and you'll be able to choose a delivery method / time that works best for you.

As always, a CaryRx team member will be available to help you through the process.
