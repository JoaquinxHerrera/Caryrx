CAREFIRST CASE STUDY

We helped improve outcomes and adherence for a leading health plan with over 60,000 members

This is how we support Health Plans like Carefirst

Ingest medical & pharmacy claims
Processing large sums of data can be challenging. CaryRx's team is made up of data analytics & pharmacy experts who can process raw data to make it easily consumable and customizable for your team.

‍A recent survey revealed that 95 percent of healthcare executives intend to transform their business digitally. However, leaders claim they are dealing with a crisis related to data readiness.¹
Utilize OneDash, our digital pharmacy & population management tool for Clinical Teams
By processing claims data, OneDash provides teams with strategic insight into data, analytics and actionable metrics to make informative decisions in real-time.

With more than 50 care managers and clinical team members using OneDash, the CareFirst team has powerful analytical tools at their finger tips, which include access to real-time HEDIS measures.
Measureable results & ROI
Utilizing OneDash for population management and CaryRx's mobile pharmacy experience for Carefirst enrollees, adherence increased by over 19% in just 12 weeks. Further, in a cohort of 248 patients, medical expenditure decreased by $900k in 6 months after implementation.

OneDash enables complex queries & predictive analytics to help you find enrollees that need direct interventions while improving outcomes.

JOSE DIAZ-LUNA

Pharmacy Services Director at CareFirst Blue Cross Blue Shield

Our focus is to utilize tools that can help improve adherence and clinical outcomes for our enrollees. Leveraging OneDash is doing just that with digital pharmacy tools for our entire case management team and clinical network. We're also excited to offer a digital pharmacy experience to our enrollees while improving collaboration amongst our clinicians.
